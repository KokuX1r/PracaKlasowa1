Split Workout Plan (2 Hours per Session)

Monday: Chest & Triceps Warm-Up (10-15 Minutes) 5-10 minutes light cardio ( treadmill, cycling) Dynamic stretches (arm circles, chest stretches) Chest Exercises (60 Minutes) Flat Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 10, 8, 6, 4 reps (increasing weight) Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 10-12 reps Chest Fly (Cable or Dumbbell) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps Push-Ups (Weighted if possible) – 3 sets to failure Triceps Exercises (30 Minutes) Close-Grip Bench Press – 4 sets x 8-10 reps Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension – 3 sets x 10-12 reps Triceps Pushdowns (Cable) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps Cool-Down (10 Minutes) Static stretches for chest and triceps Foam rolling (optional)

Wednesday: Back & Biceps

Warm-Up (10-15 Minutes) 5-10 minutes light cardio (rowing or jogging) Dynamic stretches (cat-cow stretch, arm swings) Back Exercises (60 Minutes) Deadlifts – 4 sets x 8, 6, 5, 4 reps (increasing weight) Pull-Ups/Lat Pulldown (Assisted if necessary) – 4 sets x 8-12 reps Bent-Over Barbell Rows – 4 sets x 10 reps Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets x 12 reps (each side) Biceps Exercises (30 Minutes) Barbell Bicep Curls – 4 sets x 10-12 reps Hammer Curls (Dumbbell) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps Preacher Curls (Machine or Barbell) – 3 sets x 12 reps Cool-Down (10 Minutes) Static stretches for back and biceps Foam rolling for lower back and lats

Friday: Shoulders & Legs

Warm-Up (10-15 Minutes) 5-10 minutes cardio (e.g., elliptical, jogging) Dynamic stretches (shoulder rolls, hip openers) Shoulder Exercises (45 Minutes) Overhead Barbell Press – 4 sets x 10, 8, 6, 4 reps Lateral Dumbbell Raises – 4 sets x 12-15 reps Front Dumbbell Raises – 3 sets x 12 reps Face Pulls (Cable) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps Leg Exercises (60 Minutes) Squats (Barbell or Dumbbell) – 4 sets x 10, 8, 6, 4 reps Romanian Deadlifts (RDL) – 4 sets x 10-12 reps Lunges (Walking or Stationary) – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg Leg Press (Machine) – 4 sets x 10-12 reps Calf Raises (Bodyweight or Weighted) – 4 sets x 15-20 reps Cool-Down (10 Minutes) Static stretches for shoulders, legs, and calves Foam rolling (optional)

This plan is designed to maximize hypertrophy (muscle growth) with a balanced workload. Adjust weights and repetitions based on your fitness level and goals.
